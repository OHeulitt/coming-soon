About Me

I got into web development to bring my life-long passion for inclusivity to the web. It all started with my baby sister who has special needs. She is my world and the internet should be safe and easy for her to navigate as well as anyone else. The internet is not just for hip, young people, it is for everyone!

When I am not coding, you can find me participating in local theatre, gardening, crafting, or reading books. My other loves are things pre-1982 (the older it is the more I love it) and all the nuggets: fluff, human, and chicken.
